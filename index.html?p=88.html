Recommended Hotels

 

Please note!!!

We are happy and thankful to tell you that CSWIM 2016 has got great supports and active participation from scholars and students home and abroad, but sorry to inform you  that the recommended hotel —— “Dalian University of Technology International Convention Center” has no available rooms,  please book other hotels by yourself.

Those who had booked successfully in the process of online registration wouldn’t be affected !

我们非常感谢海内外学者对于CSWIM 2016的大力支持和积极参与，同时遗憾地通知您，目前推荐酒店——大连理工大学国际会议中心已经被预订完毕，请自行联系其他酒店进行预订。

对于在注册流程中成功预订的参会者，您预订的房间不会受到影响 ！
